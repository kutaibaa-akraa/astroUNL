ملاحظة بخصوص المساعدة على الويب

July 30, 2009



For several years NAAP has provided online pretests and posttests using the EDU homework system. In the fall of 2009 the EDU system is being replaced. Unfortunately, licensing issues with the new system prevent us from continuing to provide the online assessments.


Paper based versions of the assessments are still available and can be found on the instructor's pages for each lab (the Instructor's Overview provides links to all the instructor's pages).


We apologize for any inconvenience this change will cause.
