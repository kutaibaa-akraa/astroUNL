ClassAction, Version 2

Click the button above to open the beta version of the ClassAction question browser and module editor. One of the new features of this version is the ability to create your own modules (from existing questions and resources) and download them to your computer.

How to create and download your own modules:

  • Click ‘create new module’ on the All Modules page. You can also copy an existing module by right-clicking its name.
  • Browse the ClassAction questions and resources. When you find a question (or resource) you want to include in your module, right-click (control-click on Mac) on the item's name and then select the module you want to add the question to. Removing an item from a custom module works the same way.
  • To download your modules, go to the All Modules page and click ‘download my modules’. Extract the files and open ‘start.html’ to view your questions.
  • To remove a custom module, go to the All Modules page, press and hold the shift key and then right-click (control-click on Mac) on the module name. The option to delete the module should appear in the menu. Deleting modules can not be undone at this time.
